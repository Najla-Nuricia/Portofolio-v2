## Anatomy through interaction

AR/VR Organ uses interactive 3D experiences to support learning about the human body. I designed the interface system and interaction flow for organ-focused lessons in immersive environments.

The project required readable controls, clear information hierarchy, and consistent visual feedback across Unity-based AR and VR scenes.
