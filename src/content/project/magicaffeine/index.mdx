## Everyday spaces with a magical edge

For Magicaffeine, I created pixel-art environment assets during a focused ten-day production period. The scenes support a student-made simulation game and establish its visual setting through compact, readable details.

The artwork was produced in Aseprite with attention to cohesive palettes, environmental storytelling, and game-scale clarity.
