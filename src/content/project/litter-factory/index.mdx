## Recycling at arcade speed

I designed the interface system for Litter Factory, a 3D arcade game centered on recycling. The UI communicates objectives, status, settings, and player feedback without interrupting the pace of play.

Layouts were designed in Figma and prepared for implementation in Unity, with an emphasis on quick scanning and approachable environmental education.
