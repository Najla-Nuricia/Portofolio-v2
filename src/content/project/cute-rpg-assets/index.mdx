## A small world with a soft heart

A growing collection for cheerful role-playing worlds, currently including four crops, more than 10 outdoor decorations, two buildings, and tiles for land, paths, water, and farmland. Shapes and colors are intentionally simple so scenes remain clear at game scale.

The pack is free for personal, hobby, and educational projects. Commercial use requires a paid license, credit is required, and redistribution is not permitted.
