## Nature, technology, and compact worlds

Herbatech is a one-month 2D pixel-art project exploring game environments and production-ready assets. I developed the visual language in Aseprite and used Figma to organize and present the work.

The released collection brings natural forms and technological details together in scenes designed to remain readable at game scale.
