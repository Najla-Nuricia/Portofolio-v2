## So absurd, it is cool

Blooper follows a looper worm whose search for a missing bottle becomes an unexpected journey from a sewer to Mount Merapi and finally a museum. The premise transforms a familiar Asian household meme about losing “mom’s bottle” into a humorous game objective.

Created for GAMESEED 2026, the browser-playable game combines puzzle, stealth, cozy, and single-player elements. It uses Unity, C#, and DOTween, was made without generative AI, and remains in development.
