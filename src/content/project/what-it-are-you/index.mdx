## Finding a place in technology

What IT Are You? is a four-month narrative game designed, programmed, and illustrated as a complete solo-development project. Player choices reveal personality traits and suggest an IT career that may suit them.

The released HTML5 visual novel was built in Unity with C#. Its original 2D pixel art and interface were created with Aseprite and Figma. The game is single-player, uses a fantasy post-apocalyptic setting, and was made without generative AI.
