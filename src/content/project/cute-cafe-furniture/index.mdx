## A café assembled tile by tile

A cozy 32×32 interior kit with room-building pieces, furniture, and a maid character. Warm wood, soft fabric, and tiny decorative details make each arrangement feel lived in.

The pack supports role-playing, simulation, and top-down adventure games. It was made without generative AI and is available free for personal projects, with a paid commercial license and credit required.
