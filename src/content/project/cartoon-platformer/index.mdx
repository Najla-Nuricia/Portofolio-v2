## Built to bounce

Cartoon Platformer explores energetic silhouettes, bright environments, and lively animation for compact 2D games. The 16×16 pack is designed for cheerful adventures and indie platformers, with a visual language that favors instant recognition and playful movement.

The work is still expanding. It is free for personal, hobby, and educational projects, while commercial use is available through a paid license. Credit is required, and no generative AI was used.
