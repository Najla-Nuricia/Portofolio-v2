## A bright world built for movement

Triblock Dash is a one-month 2D art project focused on environment assets for platformer and adventure gameplay. I created the pixel-art scenes and supporting visual presentation using Aseprite and Figma.

The work balances playful detail with silhouettes that remain legible while the player is moving quickly through a level.
