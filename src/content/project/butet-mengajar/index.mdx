## Learning progress made visible

I designed a family of mobile-app icons for Butet Mengajar’s gamified SNBT preparation experience. The work focused on turning study milestones and learning activities into clear, encouraging visual rewards.

The icon system was created in Figma, animated with Jitter, and prepared for lightweight in-app delivery with Lottie.
